About Our Art

Our Henna Journey

Anima Mehendi was founded with a passion for preserving the ancient art of mehendi while infusing it with contemporary designs. Our artists have over 15 years of experience creating beautiful henna designs for weddings, festivals, and special occasions.

We use only the highest quality natural henna paste, ensuring dark, long-lasting stains that are safe for all skin types. Each design is carefully crafted to reflect the unique personality and style of our clients.

From intricate bridal patterns to simple floral designs, we take pride in transforming hands and feet into works of art that tell a story.

Our Services

Bridal Mehendi

Exquisite, detailed designs for the bride, covering hands, arms, feet, and legs. Includes custom design consultation.

Party & Sangeet

Beautiful designs for wedding party members and guests. Perfect for sangeet nights and pre-wedding events.

Festival Special

Traditional designs for Karva Chauth, Eid, Diwali, and other cultural celebrations. Quick application with beautiful results.

Kids' Henna

Fun, simple designs perfect for children. Safe, natural henna that washes off more quickly than adult applications.

Home Services

Our artists will come to your home for bridal parties, family gatherings, or private events. Minimum 5 people required.

Custom Designs
